SEO vs AEO vs GEO: Which One Does Your Business Need in 2026?

By Kalindi Strategy Team•Jan 15, 2026•8 min read

The digital marketing industry has introduced a barrage of acronyms in the last few years. SEO hasn't died, but it has splintered into new disciplines. So, where should you invest your budget?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Goal: Rank in the traditional "Ten Blue Links" on Google, Bing, and other search engines.

Best for: E-commerce product pages, local businesses, and highly transactional queries where users want to compare options rather than get a single conversational answer.

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

Goal: Become the single source of truth for an AI-generated snippet (like Google AI Overviews or Alexa/Siri voice answers).

Best for: Businesses driving top-of-funnel traffic through informational queries, definitions, "how-to" articles, and direct FAQ answers.

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Goal: Get your brand mentioned, cited, and recommended in organic chat interfaces like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude.

Best for: B2B SaaS platforms, thought leaders, specialized agencies, and tools where users use LLMs for heavy research, comparisons, or recommendations.

The Verdict

You don't choose one; you execute a blended strategy. Your product pages need SEO. Your blog needs AEO. Your overall brand entity needs GEO. At Kalindi Marketing, we integrate all three into a single cohesive modern search strategy.
