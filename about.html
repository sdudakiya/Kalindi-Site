Our Story

From a Simple Idea to India's AI-Era Marketing Agency

Kalindi Marketing was born from a simple observation: digital marketing agencies were stuck in the past. Most were still running the same playbooks from 2015 — keyword stuffing, generic backlinks, and vanity metrics — while the search landscape was transforming beneath their feet.

We were founded with a single mission: to build an agency that not only masters traditional SEO, but truly understands and optimizes for the AI-powered future of search. That means Answer Engine Optimization, Generative Engine Optimization, and everything in between.

Today, we're proud to serve 200+ clients across India and globally, helping businesses of all sizes become the most visible, most trusted, and most cited brands in their industries — across Google, ChatGPT, Gemini, and beyond.
